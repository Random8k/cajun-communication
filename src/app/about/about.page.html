About

App Details

JustNews is a simple mobile news app, made to deliver relevant news articles sourced from all over. Developed as a project for an enterepenurship class, it features custom searching capability, and several built-in topic search tabs.

About the Developer

JustNews was built and programmed by Cody Dunkley, a senior at Louisiana State University. Hobbies include gaming, writing, and sleeping.
